### Keybind Names

This page lists all keybinds, what they do and their names to be used in certain functions (e.g. [sm.gui.getKeyBinding](https://scrapmechanictools.com/lua/Game-Script-Environment/Static-Functions/sm.gui#getkeybinding)).

	"Backward",					//Move backwards - S
